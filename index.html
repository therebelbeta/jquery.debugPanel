Design Debug Panel
With just a simple line of script, you can add a fully-functional design debugging panel to the site you're working on. Gather critical data instantly: window percentages, pixel widths, window size, and more. This is a must have for anyone who hard-codes websites from scratch. Simply download and include the plugin in the 
<head>
tag of your site, and get to work!
